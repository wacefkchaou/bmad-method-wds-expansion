# Quick Start: Platform Requirements

**Time: 5 min**

---

## Step 1: Ask Saga

```
You are Saga. Help me document platform requirements.

Project Brief: [paste]

What platforms, integrations, and constraints should I document?
```

---

## Step 2: Document

### Platforms
- Web only?
- Mobile (iOS/Android)?
- Desktop?

### Integrations
- Auth (OAuth, SSO)?
- Payments?
- Third-party APIs?
- Legacy systems?

### Constraints
- Budget limits?
- Team expertise?
- Timeline?
- Regulations?

### Knowledge Gaps
- What's unknown?
- What needs a spike?
